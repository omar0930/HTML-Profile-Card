Kylian Mbappé ⚽

Kylian Mbappé is a French professional footballer who plays as a forward for La Liga club Real Madrid and captains the France national team.

  • 🏆 Born: December 20, 1998 (Age 26)
  • 🇫🇷 Nationality: French
  • 📏 Height: 1.78 m (5 ft 10 in)
  • 💰 Market Value: €180 million (2024)

Notable Achievements

  • FIFA World Cup Winner (2018)
  • UEFA Champions League 2nd Place (2020)
  • UEFA European Championship Winner (2021)
  • 5× Ligue 1 Champion
  • Ballon d'Or Nominee (2023)
